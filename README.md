# :rocket: Project Name: AeroMed Delivery

## English
**Automatic medical system:** Medical store has 2 vehicles; 1 road vehicle for not urgent case and 1 Drone for emergency case. Customer must upload his prescription, urgent or not urgent option, location. If customer choose urgent then drone pick prescribed medicine and fly to customer's coordination. If customer choose not urgent then road vehicle deliver on specified location. This wholse process done automatically without involving single human.
